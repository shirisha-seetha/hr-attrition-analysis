
HR ATTRITION ANALYSIS — INSIGHTS SUMMARY
==========================================
Total Employees      : 1470
Employees Left       : 237
Overall Attrition %  : 16.12%

KEY INSIGHTS
------------
1. Sales dept has highest attrition at 20.6%
2. Age group 18-25 leaves the most at 34.8%
3. Overtime employees are 20.1% more likely to leave
4. Employees who left earned $2,046 less on average
5. Early employees (0-2 yrs) have 29.8% attrition rate

RECOMMENDATIONS
---------------
1. Focus retention on Sales department
2. Create growth paths for young employees
3. Reduce or compensate overtime fairly
4. Review and improve salary structure
5. Strengthen onboarding program
